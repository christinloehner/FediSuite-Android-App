FediSuite is a mobile client for the FediSuite Fediverse management platform.

The app lets you connect to the official hosted FediSuite instance at app.fedisuite.com or to your own self-hosted FediSuite instance.

With the Android app you can:

- connect to a specific FediSuite instance of your choice
- log in securely with instance-scoped sessions
- view your mobile dashboard and account summaries
- inspect account analytics and insights
- manage your queue of scheduled, published, failed and draft posts
- create new posts, schedule them or publish them immediately
- manage connected Fediverse accounts
- adjust language, theme, timezone and default account settings

The app is designed as a real mobile experience:

- touch-first navigation
- bottom tabs
- compact dashboard cards
- dark-mode-first UI
- explicit loading, error and empty states

FediSuite supports the idea of one central management hub for multiple Fediverse services and accounts while still keeping the app instance-based instead of locking users into one hardcoded backend.
